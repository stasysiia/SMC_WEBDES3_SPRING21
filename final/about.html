Hello, my name is Anastasiia P.

I’m a Ukrainian artist living in the USA and I love to draw aliens, some demons, and animals. I never went to school for art, but I’ve been drawing and painting for as long as I can remember. I started off drawing our family cats when I was 5 or 6 years old.

△   100% Original Artwork   △
